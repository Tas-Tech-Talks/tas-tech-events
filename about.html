This website is a hand-curated list of ICT-related events on Google Calendar, and a hand-curated list of communities or industry bodies relating somewhat to the ICT industry in Tasmania. Including Tas Game Makers, a mix of arts and technology, and SecTalks, a cyber security focused community. The page is hand curated by Leo Febey from Leo Febey Tech who has visited most of these communities or entities or met someone from them.

If you think something is missing or incorrect, don't hesitate to shoot me an email
Pull requests are also welcome on: https://github.com/Tas-Tech-Talks/tas-tech-events
